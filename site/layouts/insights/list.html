What to Do After Reading

Once an insight resonates with your situation:

  1. Identify the underlying constraint: Review the related problem page to understand the structural pattern
  2. See proof of resolution: Explore case studies that show how this constraint was addressed
  3. Understand what changes: Review outcomes that become available when this constraint is resolved

Insights sharpen your diagnosis. The diagnostic conversation determines whether the constraint is addressable in your context.
