# Anchor Document – Martin Hinshelwood

## Overview

Martin Hinshelwood is an engineering‑evolution leader and expert generalist in DevOps, Agile and Lean. His career spans more than two decades of advancing delivery performance, modernising legacy environments and strengthening technical excellence across global organisations. Martin leads Naked Agility Limited, a boutique consultancy he founded in 2013, where he designs modern product‑aligned operating models, mentors senior engineers and delivers training across DevOps, Agile, Kanban and organisational flow. He is a former technologist turned agilist who decentralises and democratises systems of work to build extraordinary processes and drive organisational change through culture, technology and teamwork. Martin is a **17‑time Microsoft MVP** (DevOps & GitHub) and a long‑standing **Professional Scrum Trainer** and **Professional Kanban Trainer**.
He authroed a "kendall Guide" and contributed to "The Open Guide to Kanban" as well as "The Scrum Guide Expanded".

## Professional Focus

Martin helps organisations adopt evidence‑based management and hypothesis‑driven development practices to boost predictability and value delivery. He integrates Agile methodologies, Scrum mastery, DevOps automation, change management and Lean principles to build continuous‑delivery systems and optimise organisational flow. As an engineering consultant, author and keynote speaker, he uses practical, no‑nonsense techniques to drive measurable improvements and has trained thousands of professionals worldwide. His work spans global enterprises, helping teams move from rigid, centralised models to adaptive, decentralised approaches that foster innovation and speed. Martin has delivered keynotes at major industry events and co‑authored _Professional ALM with Visual Studio 2012_.
